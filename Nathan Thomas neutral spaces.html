Nathan Thomas

about

Nathan Thomas is from the unincorporated community of Loudendale, West Virginia and is currently based out of Huntington. They graduated from Marshall University with a degree in journalism and minors in English and Film Studies. They write creative non-fiction essays, poetry, and short films (found under "other") with local collective Coseri. They’re on Twitter and Instagram.
